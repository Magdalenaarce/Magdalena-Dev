03 — feature I proposed

The coffee belt. Interactive.

The "cinturón del café" concept already existed in the brand — coffee only grows between the tropics. I proposed making it interactive: each origin on the world map is a hoverable point that reveals the country name, a description, and a direct link to buy that coffee. A subtle feature that reinforces the brand concept and drives product discovery.

04 — product pages

Every coffee has a story. The page tells it.

Product pages go beyond the standard Shopify template — each coffee shows its country, region, farm, altitude, producer, variety and roast profile. All structured as metadata tags, building trust and supporting SEO at the same time.

05 — b2b system

Wholesale. Without Shopify Plus.

Shopify Plus costs ~$2,000/month and is typically required for B2B features. I built the entire wholesale system — application, approval, exclusive pricing and dedicated catalog — natively, without the paid upgrade.

Application flow
Custom form for B2B requests — business details, volume, type of account.
Manual approval + activation
Admin reviews and activates access. Triggers the B2B welcome email automatically.
Exclusive pricing & catalog
B2B accounts see wholesale prices and a dedicated product catalog — no plugins.
No Shopify Plus required
Built with customer tags, metafields, and Liquid conditions. Full functionality at standard plan cost.
B2B activation email — sent automatically when access is approved. On-brand, clear, actionable.

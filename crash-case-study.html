02 — the landing

"Kein Stress, nach einem Autounfall."

The public landing explains the service, builds trust and drives users into the configurator. One clear CTA: check your claim for free. The flow is conversion-focused — hero, social proof, 3-step process, testimonials.

Desktop hero — "No stress after a car accident. Secure your compensation now." Clear value prop, direct CTA to the configurator.
03 — the configurator

Three steps. Case submitted.

The configurator guides users through their accident details in a simple, visual flow. Step by step, no legal jargon — designed so anyone can complete it regardless of their situation. Each step feeds directly into the lawyer admin.

01 role in accident
Were you the victim or at fault? Visual card selection — Geschädigter, Verursacher, Teilschuld, Unklar.
02 injury status
Any injuries? Leicht verletzt, Schwer verletzt, Unverletzt. Simple icons, no ambiguity.
03 contact details
Free, no-obligation check. Name, email, phone — case submitted to the lawyer dashboard.
also on mobile

Same flow. Any device.

The configurator works identically on mobile — same step logic, same visual clarity. Most users reach the platform after an accident, stressed and on their phone. The UX accounts for that.

mobile · step 01
Post-accident context
Users arrive stressed, often on mobile, immediately after a crash. The configurator is designed to be completable in under 2 minutes with zero friction.

04 — the admin panel

The other side: where lawyers work.

Every submitted case lands in the admin panel, where lawyers can review, assign and manage the full claim process. Case list, case detail, workflow stages — all built in Bubble.io. Data blurred for privacy.

🔒 personal data blurred
Case list view — "Fälle zuweisen" (assign cases). Tabs: open, active, closed. Search, filter, assign to lawyer.
🔒 personal data blurred
Case detail view — workflow stages: Fahrerregistrierung, Schuldklärung, Sachverständiger, Werkstatt, Geldüberweisung. Each step trackable.
05 — access control

Separate access. Secure by design.

The admin panel is protected by a dedicated login — completely separate from the public-facing configurator. Only registered lawyers can access case data.

Lawyer-only access
Email + password login, completely disconnected from the user-facing flow.
Real production use
This isn't a prototype — it's actively used by the CRASHhappens legal team to manage real accident claims in Germany.
